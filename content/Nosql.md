# NoSQL Databases

NoSQL databases provide flexible schemas and are designed for specific data models like documents, key-value pairs, graphs, or wide columns.

## When to use NoSQL

- Rapidly evolving schemas
- Large volumes of unstructured data
- High write throughput requirements
- Horizontal scaling needs
